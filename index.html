Our Story

In 1913, Haji Karimuddin established the Karim Hotel in Gali Kababian, near Jama Masjid, Delhi saying,"I want to earn fame and money by serving the royal food to the common man" This Hotel became famous because of it taste and authenticity.


Inspired by it taste "Mr. Jamal" of "AZAD PHARMACY" took the initiative along with two of his friends to bring the great taste of delhi to your city.With the support and collibration of Karim franchise , this hotel will be open short;y in sanik Market , Ranchi.
